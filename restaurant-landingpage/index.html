Our History
Discover our Glory Beginning
Divi Restaurant was founded in 2019 by Divya and Vijay, a young couple with a passion for good food and hospitality. They wanted to create a unique dining experience that celebrated fresh, locally sourced ingredients and Indian cuisine with a modern twist. After months of planning and perfecting their recipes, Divi Restaurant opened its doors in the vibrant neighborhood of [location]. The restaurant was an immediate hit thanks to Divya's creative yet comforting menu and Vijay's warm and personable service. Dishes like the Tandoori Chicken, Dal Makhani, and Mango Kulfi quickly became customer favorites. Over the years, Divi Restaurant has expanded to include a full bar serving innovative craft cocktails and an extensive wine list. They have also added an outdoor patio and live music on weekends. Divya and Vijay have maintained a hands-on approach, ensuring the highest standards for their food, service and ambiance. They have gained a loyal following of both locals and visitors who come back again and again for the restaurant's memorable experience and feel-good vibe. Divi Restaurant has become a neighborhood institution, beloved for bringing people together over delicious Indian food, lively conversation and the vision of its founders - who still greet guests at the door with warm smiles and the promise of a wonderful meal.
